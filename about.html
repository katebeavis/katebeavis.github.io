About me

I’m a Staff Software Engineer working in fintech, focused on mobile platforms, developer experience, and helping engineers ship with more confidence. I’m part of the Mobile Product tribe at Zopa, where I work across multiple squads to improve performance, clean up infrastructure, and drive technical improvements across the board.


I’m passionate about making engineers’ lives easier - I’m a big believer in working smarter, not harder (who doesn't want to spend their Friday afternoon learning something new instead of fighting CI). That’s why I care so much about automation, clean abstractions, and building tools that take the pain out of engineering. If something’s repetitive, manual, or overly complex, I want to fix it.


I enjoy sitting at the intersection of platform and product—solving messy problems, connecting the dots, and providing technical direction that helps teams move faster and make better decisions. Whether it’s shaping architecture, setting standards, or building internal tooling, I like having a hand in the bigger picture.


One of my proudest achievements was being the frontend staff engineer and technical lead for launching current accounts - a huge milestone for the company and a real growth moment for me.


Lately, I’ve been focused on improving CI/CD, abstracting our analytics layer, and supporting a large-scale migration from React Native to native mobile. I care about clean, maintainable code, thoughtful design, and building a solid, empowering engineering culture.


Outside of work, I’m into metal gigs, cooking, and learning electric guitar (slowly). I built my own gaming PC and spend far too long in character creation screens. On weekends, I’m usually exploring vegan spots around London or hosting board game nights with friends. Oh and I'm also a bit of a tube geek so if you see somebody photographing tube signs, it's probably me (140 and counting!)
